# The Prototype Bet

## What I Built
<!-- One sentence: what does this prototype demonstrate? -->
An AI-powered role-play and coaching prototype that simulates accountant–client conversations and delivers structured, voice-enabled feedback to improve advisory and client growth skills.

## The Bet in One Sentence
<!-- What you're building, for whom, why now -->
Voice-enabled AI coaching helps accountants master client conversations and grow advisory impact.

## Kill Criteria
<!-- When would you stop? What evidence would kill this bet? -->
Stop if evidence shows:

1. Low repeat usage (users don’t come back to practice)
2. Feedback feels generic vs genuinely coaching-level
3. Voice interaction doesn’t add meaningful value over text
4. No observable improvement in real-world client conversations or revenue outcomes
5. Firms won’t integrate it into workflow or justify budget

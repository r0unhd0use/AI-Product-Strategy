## Product

**Product:** Alvio-AccountingPro Coach

**What is it:** AI-powered coaching that helps accountants master client conversations and grow advisory value

**Your Role:** Investor / Co-Founder

## Scores

### Contextual Moat — _3_/5
*Workflow depth × switching cost. Would users leave in a weekend if a competitor showed up?*

**Score rationale:**
Best-practice methodology + accounting-specific scenarios creates some differentiation.  Role-play + coaching embedded into training workflows adds stickiness.
BUT: early versions are still replicable if it’s mostly prompt + content-driven. 
Switching cost increases only when:
1. It’s embedded in daily workflow
2. It tracks user progress over time
3. It integrates with firm processes


**Named attacker (from partner challenge):**
LinkedIn Learning or Coursera launching AI role-play for finance professionals



### Data Advantage — _2.5_/5
*Proprietary signal that compounds with usage. What do you see that OpenAI doesn't?*

**Score rationale:**
Strong upside if you capture:
1. Role-play transcripts
2. Coaching feedback loops
3. “What good looks like” in advisory conversations
Over time, you build a unique dataset of accountant-client interactions + outcomes
This becomes hard to replicate if:
It’s tied to real firm usage
You continuously refine best practices

The risk: If you don’t capture and structure this data → advantage disappears

**Named attacker (from partner challenge):**
Intuit (can leverage massive SMB/accountant interaction data)


### Platform Exposure — _2_/5
*Encroachment risk × pivot speed. If Apple/Google/OpenAI ships your hero feature native — then what?*

**Score rationale:**
Your core feature (AI coaching + role-play) sits directly on top of foundation models
OpenAI, Google, or Microsoft could:
Add “training mode”
Add role-play personas
Add coaching feedback natively
That would commoditize your core mechanic

Your defense:

Domain specificity (accounting workflows, compliance context)
Proprietary methodology + data
Deep embedding into tools accountants already use

**Named attacker (from partner challenge):**

OpenAI shipping native “professional coaching agents”

## Summary of diagnostic

The product does not win by features—it will win by:

Owning accountant-specific data loops, and
Embedding into real workflows, not just training
